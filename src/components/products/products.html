Silver Flight

It’s a basic flight in a tandem with a pilot in which you explore breathtaking panoramic views possible to see only from the air. We take off on one of the mounts of the Southern coast and glide over green valleys and canyons till we reach the sea and land on La Caleta beach.


Adrenaline rush, sense of freedom, fantastic views are certainly going to turn your paragliding tour in Tenerife into one of the best experiences of your life.

Golden Flight

On this flight your trained pilot will guide you through the air and use the wind currents to reach greater heights and make the flight longer. You will get to see even more gorgeous views from the height where birds fly: dramatically contrast landscapes of Tenerife, never ending ocean and neighboring islands. Takeoff and landing take place usually in the same sites as on silver flights.
