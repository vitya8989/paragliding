About us

Enjoy the thrill of flying while accompanied by our trained and experienced pilots

We are an international team of paragliding instructors with over 10 years experience in flying over Tenerife. We are proud to have Spanish and Canarian champions among us that brings us to a highest quality of service. We are passionate about our job and eager to share with others the incredible emotions that we experience every time we are in the air.


Having explored all corners of Tenerife, we have chosen the best takeoff sites and the most exciting routes to arrange tandem flights with anyone who would like to try it.


Tandem paragliding is suitable for individuals of all ages and fitness levels. No previous paragliding experience or training is required, as the pilot handles all aspects of the flight.


We pick up in the South of Tenerife for free.

Tandem flights in Tenerife

Experience the world from a new perspective!
Silver Flight

It’s a basic flight in a tandem with a pilot in which you explore breathtaking panoramic views possible to see only from the air. We take off on one of the mounts of the Southern coast and glide over green valleys and canyons till we reach the sea and land on La Caleta beach.


Adrenaline rush, sense of freedom, fantastic views are certainly going to turn your paragliding tour in Tenerife into one of the best experiences of your life.

How to book

To book paragliding in Tenerife is so simple
Choose the flight that interests you most and click “Book with 10% discount”. Fill in a simple form and proceed with payment of a deposit. You will pay the rest on the day of excursion.
Be noted

A booking of paragliding in Tenerife can be made not less than 18 hours before the flight.


If your place of stay is an apartment, you need to send us its exact address and on the day of the tandem flight, please, wait for the pick up outside of the apartment complex.


When writing your telephone number, indicate also your country code.


When you complete the booking, you will immediately get an automatic confirmation. During several hours we will also send you another email to confirm the hour and meeting point.


If you are going to book last minute, please, give us a call first.


If you stay in the North of Tenerife, you can reach us in a car. If you haven’t rented a car, we can help you book a touristic bus from Puerto de la Cruz. For that you need to call us first to agree the date.

Cancelation policy

50% refund if the tour is canceled 24 hours or earlier before the start of the tour.


No refund if the tour is canceled less than 24 hours before the start of the tour.
